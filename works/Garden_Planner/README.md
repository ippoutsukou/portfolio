# Garden Planner

A simple and intuitive web application to plan and track your garden layout across different years and seasons.

## Features

* **Interactive Grid:** Visualize your garden layout on a customizable grid. easily adjust the number of rows and columns to match your actual garden or planter boxes.
* **Seasonal Tracking:** Manage what you plant across all four seasons (Spring, Summer, Autumn, Winter) and keep a history year over year.
* **Crop Management:** 
  * Tap on the grid to add new crops.
  * Choose from a wide variety of built-in vegetable presets.
  * Register your own custom crop presets with specific colors.
  * View emojis automatically assigned based on the crop name.
* **Local Storage:** All your data, customizable grid sizes, and custom presets are saved automatically to your browser's local storage.

## UI Components

* **Grid:** Displays the current layout for the selected year and season.
* **Add Region Modal:** Allows selection of planted crops, color coding, and saving custom presets.
* **Region Detail Modal:** View and edit details of an existing planted crop. Remove crops or update their information.

## Tech Stack

* React
* TypeScript
